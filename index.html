About Us

Front End Developer

Gayatri Printers has been established since year 1997 aiming to cater the niche market in the field of manufacturing quality packaging material specialised in industrial and bulk pouches, aluminium foil etc.

Gayatri Printers has wide range of products - Zipper Pouches, Vacuum Pouches, Aluminum Foil Pouches, Medical Pouches, Aluminium foil for pharmaceutical industry, and Foil wad seal for all type of jar packing. We have plans to expand the operational network in the other part of world including Europe and American continent by year 2010. All equipments involved in manufacturing are tried and tested by the in-house specialized quality team. Periodical inspection keeps all the machines in the perfect health for the apex excellence.
